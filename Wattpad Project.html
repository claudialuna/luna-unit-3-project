Good Stories on Wattpad

Wattpad is a safe place to be able to read and write your own or other peoples stories. Readc stories from Romance to Horror to Mystery and many more. From young authors barely learning to write to adults showing just how much they know. On Wattpad you can let your imagination run wild.

The Wallflowers Revenge

The Wallflowers Revenge, tells the story of Tatiana Rostova after she is betrayed by the people that she never though would hurt her. This story is told by the point of view of Tatiana and Lucca Cavelli. This story is very beautiful, showing that even if you dont mean to you can fall in love with your savior.
