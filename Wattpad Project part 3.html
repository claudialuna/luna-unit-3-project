Instant Death

Instant Death tells the story of Selena and Ana Marke. Twin sisters living in a small town. One day Ana just... disappears. After 3 years since her sister 'death' Selena decides that its a good idea to leave to New York with her dad. There she meets Michael and they begin their friendship.. later relationship. For Ana's 5 years anniversary, Selena decides to go back home to pay her respects, but when she does she discovers a lot of things of her mother,sister, and her childhood best friend. I love how I wrote the Epilogue... and its not because I wrote it.
